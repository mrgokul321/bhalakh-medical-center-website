Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about general health, preventive care, chronic illness management, and daily wellness.

You should consult a general physician for fever, infections, fatigue, chronic conditions, allergies, digestive issues, headaches, or any symptoms affecting your overall health.

A yearly health check-up is recommended for most adults. People with diabetes, hypertension, heart issues, or other chronic conditions may require more frequent monitoring.

General physicians treat a wide range of conditions including infections, diabetes, hypertension, thyroid issues, respiratory problems, digestive disorders, and general illnesses.

A general physician is your first point of contact. They diagnose and treat most conditions and refer you to a specialist only when necessary for advanced evaluation or procedures.

Eating a balanced diet, regular exercise, proper sleep, hydration, stress control, and periodic medical check-ups help maintain strong immunity and overall well-being.
