Radiology

Radiology is a medical speciality that deals with the use of imaging techniques for the diagnosis and treatment of disease by providing diagnostic imaging services to our patients. Our Radiologists are specialized in this medical field and provide accurate diagnosis through imaging techniques such as X-rays, ultrasounds, CT scans, MRIs, mammograms etc.

Orthopaedics

Orthobiologics

Orthobiologics is a branch of regenerative medicine which uses patient’s own cells to heal and recover musculoskeletal injuries at a faster pace and in a natural way. The Orthobiology department at our medical centre has a team of trained Orthobiology Consultants who have experience in dealing with all kinds of musculoskeletal disorders, including arthritis and tendonitis.

Paediatrics

Paediatrics is a medical speciality that deals with the care of infants, children, and adolescents. Our Paediatric consultants have specialized and provide patients with high-quality care. They also help parents understand their children’s health conditions and provide them with information about how to take care of their kids.

Pulmology

Pulmonology deals with diseases involving the respiratory tract. The respiratory tract includes the nose, mouth, throat, and lungs. Our consultant Pulmonologist is one of the most experienced people in the field who effectively diagnoses, treat, and manage patients with conditions involving the lungs or breathing problems such as asthma or COPD.

Know More

Medical Gastroenterology

Our surgical gastroenterologist is specialized in the surgical treatment of diseases of the gastrointestinal tract. They specialize in minimally invasive procedures, such as laparoscopic surgery, robotic surgery and endoscopy. Our medical centre is well equipped for your visit from consultation to post-surgery care.
