About Bhalakh Medical Centre

Bhalakh Medical Centre is a sophisticated day-care medical centre with refined infrastructure in Adyar, Chennai, with the most advanced diagnostic equipment and Consultants from different departments offering the finest medical care.

Bhalakh Medical Centre was established in 2022 by Dr Ranjitha and Dr Udhayashankar to provide advanced medical services and create healing experiences for patients. The medical centre has the best Consultants in town, who along with Dr Ranjitha and Dr Udhayashankar offer top notch treatment that focuses on current medical advancements and individual needs.

Located in the heart of Chennai city, the centre has an advanced approach to healthcare, latest screening and testing equipment, examination rooms and a patient-friendly environment for speedy recovery that aims to create a comfortable and pleasant experience for every patient.

Vision

To bring the humane back to healthcare and create an experience for patients to take back as they heal.

Mission

To have a well-defined scope of work and a patient-first approach. To provide patients and visitors with an overall great experience so they are utmost satisfied with the care and treatment.
