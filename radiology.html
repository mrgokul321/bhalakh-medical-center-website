Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions our patients ask about radiology tests, scans, and imaging procedures.

No, radiology imaging tests such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs are painless. You will only need to stay still during the procedure for clear images.

Radiation levels used in modern machines are very low and safe for diagnostic purposes. Doctors only recommend scans when necessary to ensure patient safety.

CT scans typically take 5–15 minutes, while MRI scans may take 20–45 minutes depending on the body part being examined.

For some tests—like abdominal ultrasound or contrast CT—fasting may be required. Our team will inform you in advance if any preparation is needed.

Most reports like X-rays and ultrasounds are available on the same day. CT and MRI reports may take 12–24 hours depending on complexity.
