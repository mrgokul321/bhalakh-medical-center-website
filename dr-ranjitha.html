Managing Director

Dr Ranjitha Sudanthiram , M.D.R.D

Managing Director, Bhalakh Medical Centre

“The true meaning of success is how much and well I can give back to my community and how many lives I have enhanced”

Dr Ranjitha is a highly accomplished radiologist, educator and an expert in her department who strives to be updated with the advancements in the field of radiology. Her passion led her to pursue studies of medicine as her major. Dr Ranjitha has a particular interest in women's imaging and ultrasound-guided interventional procedures. She always keeps her eyes open about different medical aspects and procedures to provide her patients with the best care.

Dr Ranjitha completed her schooling at Chettinad Vidyashram and Rotary Matric and received her MBBS degree and MD in Radiology from Sri Ramachandra Medical College. She later went on to complete a fellowship at the prestigious King's College where she gained valuable expertise in the field of radiology.

Her knowledge of the best of both worlds led her to develop her revolutionary multi-specialty care centre in Adyar, Chennai. She now caters to both conventional methods as well as innovative techniques in Chennai.

Beyond the above, Dr Ranjitha also has a passion for giving back to the community and giving young people the opportunity to have a chance at success. She actively participates in school educational programs to raise awareness among girls and caregivers about breast cancer prevention, early cervical cancer detection, and menstrual hygiene.
