Medical Director

Dr S Udhayashankar , M.S. Ortho, IMGSS (UK)

Medical Director, Bhalakh Medical Centre

“I wish to use every opportunity to not only save lives but also heal lives and bring up a society where health is prioritized”

Dr Udhayashankar is a renowned Orthopaedic Surgeon in Chennai with a strong commitment to providing innovative, cutting-edge surgical procedures that enhance the lives of his patients.

His interest in new technologies began during his student days and continues to influence his approach to modern orthopaedic care. He completed his Bachelor’s degree at Sri Ramachandra Medical College and later earned his Master of Surgery in Orthopaedics at Muthiah Medical College, Chidambaram.

He has been actively involved in the diagnosis and treatment of complex orthopaedic conditions and has undergone advanced training in arthroscopy, sports injuries, trauma care and joint replacement surgeries.

Dr Udhayashankar has worked with leading institutions and hospitals, gaining extensive experience in patient care, surgical excellence and post-operative rehabilitation.

At Bhalakh Medical Centre, he has been responsible for strengthening the orthopaedic department by integrating advanced technologies, evidence-based practices and a patient-first approach.

Experience & Achievements

  • • Extensive experience in orthopaedic trauma, sports injuries and joint replacement procedures.
  • • Advanced training in arthroscopic surgeries and minimally invasive orthopaedic techniques.
  • • Actively involved in adopting modern surgical technologies to improve patient outcomes and recovery time.
  • • Recognized for clinical excellence, ethical practice and leadership in orthopaedic care.
